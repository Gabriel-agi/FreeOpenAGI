Dictionary Anatomy

Dictionaries store key-value pairs. Click on the different parts of the dictionary below to identify them.

{"name": "Atlas", "version": 2.1, "status": "active"}

Identify all parts!

Opening curly brace `{`: Starts the dictionary. A Key: Unique identifier for a value (often a string). Colon `:`: Separates a key from its value. A Value (String): The data associated with a key. A Value (Number): Data can be numbers, lists, or even other dictionaries! Closing curly brace `}`: Ends the dictionary.
